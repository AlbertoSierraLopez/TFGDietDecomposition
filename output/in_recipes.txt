1.Preheat the oven 400f, in 8 or 9 inch square baking dish arrange half the potatoes, season with salt and pepper and then sprinkle with nutmeg, top with onion and apple, arrange the remaining potatoes on top, season with salt and pepper, add milk, cover with foil, bake for 30 to 40 minutes or until the potatoes are tender, top with cheese and bake uncovered 10 to 15 minutes or until lightly browned.
